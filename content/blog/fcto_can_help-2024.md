As a technology leader or CEO are you burdened by any of these problems?

- Software Engineering team(s) under-performing relative to your expectations.
- Challenges in implementing technological features and fostering innovation. 
- Burdened with technical debt or a legacy system that is hindering your progress.
- About to take on a technical challenge but you don’t have the right leadership in place to confidently make it happen.

There are many ways to go about solving these kinds of problems, but ultimately they all require experienced leadership. I can help.

Bringing me on as your Fractional CTO can provide your organization with several key benefits:

Strategic leadership: I will bring a wealth of experience and expertise to your team, helping you develop and execute a technology strategy that aligns with your business goals.

Innovation and agility: I will challenge the status quo and drive innovation within your technology team, ensuring that you stay ahead of the competition.

Technical expertise: I have deep technical knowledge and experience, enabling me to provide guidance and support to your engineering team, helping them deliver high-quality software solutions.

Cost-Effectiveness: Hiring a Fractional CTO is a cost-effective way to access the skills and experience of a seasoned technology leader without the need for a full-time executive.

Focus on your core business: By outsourcing your technology leadership, you can free up your internal resources to focus on your core business activities.

Access to a network of experts: I have a vast network of industry contacts and can leverage my relationships to help you find the right talent and solutions for your technology needs. This includes both subject-matter experts as well as firms that can accelerate your technology objectives.

Objectivity and fresh perspective: As an external consultant, I will provide an objective perspective and fresh ideas, helping you identify areas for improvement and make informed decisions about your technology strategy.

Even if you are unsure that a Fractional CTO is the right thing for you, reach out and let’s find out.
